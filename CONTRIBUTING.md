# Contributing

`git-manager` is not currently open to external contributions.

The project is maintained solely by [@Tlahey](https://github.com/Tlahey) at this stage. Pull requests will not be reviewed or merged for now.

This will change as the project matures — contribution guidelines will be published here once the project opens up to outside contributors.

In the meantime, feel free to fork the repository for your own use under the terms of the [LICENSE](LICENSE).
